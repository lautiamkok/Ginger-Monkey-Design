Rare Penny takes its name from a symbol of rarity: the 1930 Australian penny. Just as that coin became a cherished heirloom, our wines are crafted to be discovered, savoured, and remembered. From the rolling vineyards of South Australia, each bottle carries the weight of history and the joy of sharing something truly special.

160 years of winemaking, poured into every glass.

From the Kulara Estate’s first vines to modern vintages, Rare Penny carries history forward.

Each Rare Penny wine is crafted with care, balancing heritage with modern taste.
