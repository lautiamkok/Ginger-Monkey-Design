Every heirloom has a story. Ours begins in 1856.

At Kulara Estate, winemaking has been passed down for over 160 years. Through droughts, harvests, and generations, the land has given us grapes of rare quality. Rare Penny takes its name from the 1930 penny — a coin struck in the shadows of the Great Depression, unnoticed at first, then treasured by families across Australia.

Like that coin, our wines are more than something to enjoy; they are heirlooms of flavour, crafted with care, meant to be shared and passed on.
